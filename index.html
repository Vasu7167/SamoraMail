LOSS PATTERNS — WHAT KILLS DEALS
26d
The silent death window

The average deal goes cold 26 days before a manager notices. In that window, the rep is still logging it as active. The CRM still shows it green. The forecast still counts it. SAM sees it on day 4.

→ Cold thread alert triggered at day 7 of no omnichannel activity. Not day 26.

Churn rate after single proposal open

When a proposal is opened only once and there's no calendar action within 7 days, 78% of those deals are lost. One open is curiosity. Multiple opens with calendar follow-through is buying intent.

→ SAM downgrades pipeline probability immediately after day 7 of single-open silence.
2×
Objection repetition is a product problem

When the same objection appears in 2+ meeting transcripts across different reps and different accounts, it is a product or positioning gap — not a rep skill issue. SAM surfaces it within the same week.

→ Routed to product team as structured feedback. Not lost in an email thread.
