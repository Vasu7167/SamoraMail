How It Works

From inbox to insight
in seconds.

No manual data entry. No rep reporting. Connect your channels once — SAM reads everything automatically.

01

Connect your channels via OAuth

Each rep connects Gmail, LinkedIn and WhatsApp once during onboarding — one click per channel. OAuth tokens are stored securely. No passwords, no manual exports, no CSV uploads. Done once, works forever.

02

Search any account — instantly

Type a company or contact name. SAM scans every channel simultaneously — sent emails, LinkedIn threads, WhatsApp conversations — and pulls the full omnichannel history for that account in seconds.

03

AI reads every thread, every channel

Two-stage AI analysis reads what was said, how they responded, how fast, what sentiment, which channel they prefer. Generates a unified Hot / Warm / Cold signal with a precise relationship assessment and one specific next action.

04

Task vs reality — verified automatically

Rep logged "Follow up Ferrero" as today's task. SAM checks every channel. Last email: 26 days ago. LinkedIn: no message. WhatsApp: no thread. The gap is surfaced to the manager — before the pipeline review, not at it.

05

Manager sees every rep's signals

One dashboard. All reps. All channels. Every signal unified. No rep self-reporting. No "I think I followed up." Org-level signal intelligence — configurable per company based on sales cycle, team type and channel mix.
